Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through our online portal, mobile app, or by calling our customer service line. Our online booking system allows you to select your preferred doctor, date, and time.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and Medicare. Please contact our billing department for specific information about your insurance coverage.

How do I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our secure patient portal. Simply log in with your credentials, and you'll be able to view your test results, doctor's notes, and treatment plans.

How does telemedicine work?

Our telemedicine service allows you to consult with healthcare providers via video call. Simply schedule an appointment, and you'll receive a secure link to join the virtual consultation at the appointed time.
